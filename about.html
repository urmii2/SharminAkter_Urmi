About 100SMILES Dental Care

Located at 33 Fantail St., Razole, 100SMILES Dental Care has been serving the community for over three decades. With more than 50 years of combined experience, our clinic provides friendly, reliable, and compassionate dental care for patients of all ages.

We offer a comprehensive range of services, from routine check-ups and cleanings to advanced dental implants, orthodontics, and cosmetic dentistry. Our focus is on preventive care, patient education, and ensuring a positive experience for everyone.
