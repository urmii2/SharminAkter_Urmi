Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I book an appointment?

A: You can book online via our booking system, call us at 07 1327 5599, or email admin@100smilesdentalcare.com.au.

Q: What services are included in the Annual Dental Check-Up?

A: The check-up includes physical examination, cleaning, X-rays, dental evaluation, nutritional guidance, and additional lab work if required.

Q: Are your services suitable for children?

A: Yes! We have a dedicated pediatric dentist and child-friendly treatments.

Q: Do you offer emergency dental services?

A: Yes, we provide emergency dental care for urgent issues. Call us immediately if needed.
